HOW TO SAVE YOUR DOSSIER

■ SAVING & LOADING YOUR CHARACTER

Your Operative Dossier automatically saves your progress in your browser's local storage — so if you reload the page on the same device, your work will be restored.

To save a permanent file or share your character, click ↓ SAVE JSON in the toolbar at the bottom of the character sheet. This downloads a .json file you can keep anywhere.

To load a saved character on any device or browser, click ↑ LOAD JSON and select your saved file. Your full character state — attributes, skills, SPIKE level, HP, and all notes — will be restored instantly.

To start fresh, click ✕ RESET. You'll be asked to confirm before anything is cleared.
