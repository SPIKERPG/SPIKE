// 01 CORE ROLLS & PROTOCOLS
■ THE CORE ROLL
d10 + ATTRIBUTE + SKILL ≥ TARGET NUMBER
Untrainedd10 + ATTRIBUTE only (no skill bonus)
Trained+2 to roll
Specialized+3 to roll (via advancement)
■ TARGET NUMBERS
TN 9Standard
TN 11Cover / Difficult
TN 13Heavy Cover / Extreme
■ DICE USED
d10All controlled actions — combat, skills, movement, technical
d20SPIKE & emotional rolls — instability, recovery, behavior table
■ CRITICAL RESULTS (d10)
NAT 10CRIT SUCCESS +2 damage OR narrative bonus — situation swings in your favor
NAT 1CRIT FAULT Complication arises — jam, noise, exposure, unintended consequence
■ ADVANTAGE / DISADVANTAGE
AdvantageRoll 2 dice of the relevant type. Take the higher.
DisadvantageRoll 2 dice of the relevant type. Take the lower.
■ RESTS
Short Rest SPIKE –1. Roll Quick Recovery if applicable.
Long Rest HP + COMPOSURE to max. SPIKE –2.
■ VITALS FORMULA
HP = 10 + BODY
COMPOSURE = 5 + RESOLVE
■ COMPOSURE THRESHOLDS
COMPOSURE 1 –1 to all BODY checks
COMPOSURE 0 Immediate SPIKE Save — d20 + RESOLVE vs TN 14. While at 0: –1 to all rolls until COMPOSURE is restored.
// 02 COMBAT OPERATIONS
■ TURN STRUCTURE
1 ActionAttack, assist, use a skill, technical action, grounding attempt
1 Minor ActionMove 1 zone, draw a weapon, quick interaction

Actions and Minor Actions may be used in any order.

■ INITIATIVE
d10 + AWARENESS

Act in descending order. Ties: player preference or GM call. Fixed for the encounter.

■ ATTACK RESOLUTION
d10 + ATTRIBUTE + SKILL vs DEFENSE TN

On hit: roll weapon damage. Armor reduces incoming damage by flat amount.

■ REACTIONS — 2 PER OPERATIVE / LONG REST
OverwatchShoot enemy entering your line of sight. Requires Aimed action last turn. (Ranged)
CounterattackImmediate return strike if attacked in close combat. (Melee only)
ProtectInterpose yourself to take a hit meant for an adjacent ally.
BraceReduce all incoming damage from the triggering hit by 2.
■ ENGAGEMENT ZONES
CLOSEMelee range. Direct physical contact.
NEARPistol range. Short engagement distance.
FARRifle range. Long engagement distance.
OUT OF REACHExtreme distance. Sniper-class only.
■ MOVEMENT
StandardMove 1 zone as a Minor Action. No cost.
Extended ×1Move 2 zones as Minor Action: –1 COMPOSURE.
Extended ×2Move 2 zones as full Action: no COMPOSURE penalty.
■ WEAPONS
Light 1d10 dmg Pistol, knife, baton. Flexible, concealable.
Standard 2d10 dmg Rifle, shotgun, heavy melee. Balanced.
Heavy 3d10 dmg Sniper, LMG. Cannot move + attack same turn.
■ ARMOR
Light –2 incoming dmg No penalty.
Medium –4 incoming dmg –1 Mobility checks.
// 03 THE SPIKE SCALE
■ SPIKE RESONANCE WARNING

When two individuals' SPIKE levels differ by 4 or more, the lower-SPIKE individual experiences discomfort in proximity. Both suffer Disadvantage on social checks while the gap persists. Log all resonance events upon return.

LVL DESIGNATION MECHANICAL EFFECT SPIKE SAVE
0 SERENE +1 to logic and TECH rolls. Cannot critically fail SPIKE Saves. —
1–2 STABLE No mechanical effect. Nominal baseline. —
3–5 SHAKEN –1 to all MIND rolls. d20 + RESOLVE vs TN 9 when triggered
6–7 CRACKED –1 to MIND and RESOLVE. Hallucinations/memory distortion possible. Save required when triggered
8–9 OVERLOADED Disadvantage on all social checks. Non-compliant in roleplay. SPIKE Save required hourly
10 SPIKED Loss of character control. Roll Behavior Table (d20) at start of each turn. End of turn: d20 + RESOLVE vs TN 17. Nat 20: SPIKE –2. Nat 1: SPIKE +1.
11 CONSUMED 1d6 True Damage/turn until HP 0, then 1d6 RESOLVE dmg/turn until 0. Death follows. End of turn: d20 + RESOLVE vs TN 19. Nat 1: damage rolls at Advantage.
■ LOWERING SPIKE
Short RestSPIKE –1
Long RestSPIKE –2
Grounding Once/encounter: Action + d20 + MIND vs TN 13. Success: SPIKE –1. Ally at SPIKE 4 or lower may grant Advantage.

Teammate grounds SPIKED operative via logic, routine, or calm command. Requires an Action + roleplay.
